About Me

Turning data into decisions

I came to data through public health. Before the MSc, I spent five years in federal roles at the CDC and AmeriCorps, where I collected and validated clinical data, managed compliance with agencies, universities, and community organizations. That work taught me something most analysts learn later: data only matters if it reaches the right person in a form they can act on.

The MSc in Computer Science formalized the technical side: pipelines, modeling, databases, full-stack development. The public health background is what keeps me focused on the question behind the query.
